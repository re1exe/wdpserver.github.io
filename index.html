🌟 About Us 🌟

Welcome to WDP Minecraft – a fun and competitive SMP hosted in the heart of Europe. Whether you’re here to explore, build, or test your skills against others, every adventure counts and every player has a chance to shine.

🎮 Version: 1.21.4 (all versions supported)
📍 Location: Belgium – great connection for EU players
🟢 Status: Online 24/7
🤝 Community: Active, welcoming & competitive
💬 Discord: Join WDP Discord

✨ Features ✨

  • 🌐 Cross-version access – join from any Minecraft version
  • 📢 Discord integration – updates, support & in-game chat
  • ⚖️ Fair play – no pay-to-win, just pure gameplay
  • 🔄 Regular updates – new content & improvements

Whether you’re here to make friends, test your skills, or leave your mark on the world, WDP is your place to play, compete, and have fun. 🌍
